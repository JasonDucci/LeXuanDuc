An orphan's war

Author: Molly Green

Genre: Fiction

Published: April 23, 2018

War rages, but the women and children of Liverpool's Dr Barnardo's Home cannot give up hope. A gripping saga about love and loss on the Home Front. When her childhood sweetheart Johnny is killed in action, Maxine Grey loses more than her husband she loses her best friend. Desperate to make a difference in this awful war, Maxine takes a nursing job at London's St Thomas's Hospital.
