The Cloud Mystery

Author: Joseph Lafountain

Genre: Fiction

Published: May 10, 2015

With a stunning cover design that captures the essence of this thrilling adventure, this science fiction novel is perfect for fans of intergalactic travel and mind-bending technology. Get ready to be transported to a world beyond your imagination.
