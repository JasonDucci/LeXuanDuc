To make a King

Author: Kristi L. Cramer

Genre: Fiction/Fantasy

Published: Sep 27, 2017

To Make a King is a Young Adult Fantasy in three parts. Part One: A True Prince Two friends discover the key to a mystery that, once unlocked, will change everything. Enemies they didn't know they had unleash magical forces to make sure the secret never comes to light. Part Two: To Make a King Dark magic undermines the Crown Prince's succession, and a young prince is encouraged to take action against his half-brother in a plot to usurp the crown. Before the sun sets on coronation day, brother will face brother and swords will be bared. Part Three: Should Monarchs Stumble When the new king stumbles, an old enemy is quick to take advantage, conspiring to take the kingdom by force. The king’s jester must decide whether to back the usurper or help the king choose to be a better man.
